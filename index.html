Interactive Memory Game

follow the colours to glory, and high scores!

The aim of the game is to press the corresponding button in the randomly generated run of colours.
The runs will start off nice and simple (only four colours), but will get progressively harder (up to ten colours).
You have two rounds of each length, when you hit ten you'll have to see how long you can hold out.
Enjoy! And don't forget to share your high scores!
